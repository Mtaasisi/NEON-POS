Step-by-step guide to test IMEI/Serial number creation through the web interface

Step 1: Open Application

  1. Open your browser
  2. Navigate to: http://localhost:5173 (or your app URL)
  3. Login with your credentials
Open App ‚Üí

Step 2: Navigate to Purchase Orders

  1. Click "Purchase Orders" in the left menu
  2. Either create a new PO or find an existing one
  3. Make sure PO status is: "Sent", "Confirmed", or "Shipped"

Step 3: Add Items to Purchase Order

  1. Click "Add Item" button
  2. Select a Product (e.g., iPhone, Samsung)
  3. Select a Variant (e.g., "128GB", "Black") - This becomes the parent variant
  4. Enter Quantity: 3 (for testing)
  5. Enter Unit Cost: 150
  6. Click "Save" or "Add"

Step 4: Receive Purchase Order

  1. On the PO detail page, click "Receive Items"
  2. Select "Full Receive" or "Partial Receive"
  3. Click "Proceed to Receive"

Step 5: Enter IMEI/Serial Numbers

Enter IMEI or Serial numbers (one per item):

üìù Test Data (Copy & Paste):

IMEI 1: 123456789012345 IMEI 2: 234567890123456 IMEI 3: 345678901234567

OR use Serial Numbers:

Serial 1: SN-TEST-001 Serial 2: SN-TEST-002 Serial 3: SN-TEST-003
  • Fill in Cost Price (auto-filled from PO)
  • Fill in Selling Price (optional)
  • Set Condition: "new" (default)
  • Add Location (optional)
  • Add Notes (optional)

Step 6: Set Pricing & Confirm

  1. Review pricing for each item (if prompted)
  2. Adjust if needed
  3. Click "Confirm" or "Save"
  4. Click "Confirm Receive" to complete

‚úÖ Step 7: Verify Results

  1. Purchase Order: Status should be "Received"
  2. Inventory Page: Navigate to Inventory
  3. Find the product you received
  4. You should see:
    • Parent variant with quantity = 3
    • 3 child variants with individual IMEI/Serial numbers
    • Each child variant is active and available
