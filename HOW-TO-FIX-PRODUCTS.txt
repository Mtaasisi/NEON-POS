Step 2: Login with these credentials:

        Password: 123456

Step 3: Press F12 (or Fn+F12 on Mac) to open Developer Console
        (Or right-click → Inspect → Console tab)

Step 4: Open the file: QUICK-FIX-PRODUCTS.js
        (It's in your project root folder)
